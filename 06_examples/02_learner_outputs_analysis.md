# 02 Learner Outputs Analysis — Urban Linguistic Landscape OER

## 🧠 Overview

This document provides a framework for analyzing learner-generated artefacts within the **Urban Linguistic Landscape OER** intervention. The aim is to explore how **linguistic mediation**, **multimodal production**, and **AR-based recontextualization** manifest in the learners' work, and to assess how well these outputs align with the OER's pedagogical objectives.

This analysis framework is designed to complement the other modules, such as:

- **[01 Teaching Design](../01_teaching_design/README.md)**: For understanding the pedagogical design underlying the artefacts.
- **[02 Learning Tasks](../02_learning_tasks/README.md)**: To link the learner artefacts to the task-based activities designed for urban linguistic exploration.
- **[03 Assessment](../03_assessment/README.md)**: For evaluating the quality of the learner outputs based on predefined criteria.
- **[04 AR Guidelines](../04_ar_guidelines/README.md)**: To understand the integration of Augmented Reality (AR) in learning activities.

## 🧩 Analytical Framework

The analysis of learner outputs should be guided by the following dimensions:

### 1. **Linguistic Mediation**
- **Objective**: Evaluate how learners transform and mediate meaning between languages (e.g., from L1 to L2).
- **Focus**: Accuracy, clarity, and adaptation for the target audience.
- **Indicators**: Grammatical correctness, vocabulary selection, sentence structure, and pragmatics.

### 2. **Multimodal Production**
- **Objective**: Assess how learners integrate various modes of communication (text, images, audio) to convey meaning.
- **Focus**: Coherence, appropriateness of modality selection, and the synergy between different modes.
- **Indicators**: Visual design, clarity of text and image integration, effective use of supplementary modes (audio, video).

### 3. **AR Integration (Optional)**
- **Objective**: Determine how learners incorporate **Augmented Reality (AR)** to extend learning into real-world, spatial environments.
- **Focus**: Relevance of AR integration, seamlessness of AR tools with the physical space, and pedagogical impact.
- **Indicators**: Ease of access (QR codes, geolocation markers), interaction design, spatial learning alignment.

## 🧩 Example of Learner Output Analysis

### Artefact: Urban Sign Mediation

| **Dimension**             | **Analysis**                                                                                           |
|---------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Linguistic Mediation**   | The student successfully simplifies the message, translating it to Chinese with minimal errors, but some idiomatic phrases lack full clarity. |
| **Multimodal Production**  | The sign image is well-integrated with the Chinese text, but the color contrast could be enhanced for better readability. |
| **AR Integration**         | The AR feature works well, but the physical location could be better linked to the digital content for a richer learning experience. |
