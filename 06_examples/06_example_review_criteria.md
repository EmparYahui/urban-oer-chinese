# 06 Example Review Criteria — Urban Linguistic Landscape OER

## 🧠 Overview

This document provides the **review criteria** to evaluate learner-generated artefacts within the **Urban Linguistic Landscape OER**. The goal is to assess how well each artefact meets the intended educational goals, focusing on **linguistic mediation**, **multimodal production**, **AR integration**, and **OER potential**.

---

## 🧩 Review Criteria

### 1. **Linguistic Accuracy**
- **Criteria**: Evaluate the grammatical correctness and the appropriateness of vocabulary used in the artefact.
- **Rating Scale**:
  - Excellent: No errors, fluent expression.
  - Good: Minor errors, but meaning is clear.
  - Needs Improvement: Errors that hinder understanding.

### 2. **Multimodal Integration**
- **Criteria**: Assess the coherence and integration of text, images, audio, or other modes used in the artefact.
- **Rating Scale**:
  - Excellent: Seamless integration of modes.
  - Good: Clear integration, but some improvements could be made.
  - Needs Improvement: Poor integration of modes, leading to confusion.

### 3. **Cultural Sensitivity**
- **Criteria**: Evaluate how well the artefact takes into account the cultural context and adapts urban input for the target audience.
- **Rating Scale**:
  - Excellent: High cultural awareness and accurate adaptation.
  - Good: Adequate cultural context, minor issues.
  - Needs Improvement: Lack of cultural sensitivity or contextual adaptation.

### 4. **AR Integration**
- **Criteria**: Assess how well AR is used in the artefact and whether it enhances the learning experience.
- **Rating Scale**:
  - Excellent: Effective AR integration, enriching the learning experience.
  - Good: Functional AR, but could be more interactive or immersive.
  - Needs Improvement: AR is either missing or not fully functional.

### 5. **OER Potential**
- **Criteria**: Evaluate the reusability and adaptability of the artefact for different contexts and learners.
- **Rating Scale**:
  - Excellent: Highly reusable and adaptable to various contexts.
  - Good: Usable with some minor adjustments.
  - Needs Improvement: Limited reuse potential.

---

## 🧩 Purpose

These criteria ensure that all learner-generated artefacts are evaluated consistently and align with the **Urban Linguistic Landscape OER** pedagogical goals.

---

## 🧩 Notes for Adaptation

- Review criteria can be simplified or expanded depending on the context and student level.
- Can be used to give formative feedback during the learning process.
