# 04 Example Reusability — Urban Linguistic Landscape OER

## 🧠 Overview

This document explores how artefacts generated within the **Urban Linguistic Landscape OER** can be reused and adapted in different educational contexts. It provides insights on how these artefacts can be leveraged for ongoing learning, collaboration, and knowledge sharing.

---

## 🧩 Reusability Criteria

### 1. **Pedagogical Reusability**
- How adaptable is the artefact for different levels of proficiency (e.g., A1, A2)?
- Can the artefact be modified for use in different cultural or linguistic contexts?

### 2. **Content Reusability**
- Are the core elements of the artefact flexible enough to be applied to various teaching scenarios?
- Can the artefact be updated to reflect new learning outcomes or concepts?

### 3. **Multimodal Reusability**
- Can the multimodal components (text, images, audio) be independently modified or reused in other tasks?
- Are the modes of communication integrated in a way that facilitates reuse in different contexts?

### 4. **AR Reusability**
- If the artefact includes AR or geolocation features, can these be adapted to different urban environments or subjects?
- Are there alternative AR tools that could be used to enhance the artefact's educational value?

---

## 🧩 Example of Reusability

**Artefact**: Urban Sign Mediation

- **Pedagogical Reusability**: The artefact, which transforms an urban sign into a Chinese language learning resource, can be adapted to other urban signs and reused at different proficiency levels (A1 to A2).
- **Content Reusability**: The bilingual nature of the artefact (L1 → L2) allows for modification and adaptation to other linguistic contexts.
- **Multimodal Reusability**: The image-text combination is highly adaptable, allowing easy integration of additional modes (e.g., audio or video).
- **AR Reusability**: The QR code can be adapted to link to different locations, and AR tools can be modified to integrate with different types of urban input (e.g., public transportation signs, restaurant menus).
