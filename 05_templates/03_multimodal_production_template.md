# 03 Multimodal Production Template — Urban Linguistic Landscape OER

## Overview

This template guides the production of **OER-ready multimodal artefacts**. It integrates text, image, audio, and video to represent linguistic and cultural meaning through multiple modes.

---

## Template Structure

| **Component**         | **Description**                                                                                      |
|-----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Artefact title**     | Provide a title for the artefact.                                                                    |
| **Core message**       | Define the central message of the artefact.                                                          |
| **Text component**     | Provide the written content in Chinese, specifying the linguistic focus.                             |
| **Visual component**   | Describe any visual elements (images, graphics) and their relevance.                                |
| **Optional audio/video**| Include any audio or video elements, and their role in conveying the message.                        |
| **Design rationale**   | Explain why particular modes and elements were used.                                                 |
| **Intended audience**  | Specify the intended audience for the artefact (e.g., learners, teachers).                           |
| **Reuse potential**    | Assess how the artefact can be reused or adapted across different contexts.                          |

---

## Purpose

This template ensures multimodal artefacts meet pedagogical goals and are aligned with the **Urban Linguistic Landscape OER** framework.

---

## Notes for Adaptation

- Suitable for a range of multimodal outputs (e.g., posters, digital artefacts, videos).
- Can be simplified or expanded depending on learners' needs.
