# 04 Assessment Template — Urban Linguistic Landscape OER

## Overview

This template provides a structured format for designing assessments aligned with the multimodal rubrics. It supports evaluating learner artefacts in terms of **linguistic clarity**, **mediation quality**, **multimodal production**, **OER potential**, and **reflective quality**.

---

## Template Structure

| **Assessment Component**  | **Description**                                                                                      |
|---------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Assessment title**       | Provide a title for the assessment.                                                                  |
| **Objective**              | Define the purpose of the assessment (e.g., evaluating linguistic competence, mediation skills).       |
| **Assessment dimensions**  | **Linguistic clarity**: Evaluate linguistic accuracy (grammar, vocabulary).<br>**Mediation quality**: Assess the adaptation and transformation of meaning.<br>**Multimodal production**: Evaluate coherence and integration of text, images, and audio.<br>**OER potential**: Assess the reusability and adaptability of the artefact as OER.<br>**Reflective quality**: Evaluate the learner's ability to reflect on the learning process and contextual adaptation. |
| **Grading rubric**         | See the [rubrics for each dimension](../03_assessment/02_rubrics_multimodal.md) for detailed evaluation criteria. |
| **Feedback criteria**      | Provide feedback for each dimension, focusing on strengths and areas for improvement.               |

---

## Purpose

This template ensures standardized assessment practices, aligned with the pedagogical framework of the OER, ensuring consistency and fairness in evaluation.

---

## Rubrics

For a comprehensive evaluation, refer to the following rubrics, which assess the key dimensions of multimodal learner artefacts:  
- **Linguistic Clarity**
- **Mediation Quality**
- **Multimodal Production**
- **OER Potential**
- **Reflective Quality**

You can find detailed rubrics for each of these dimensions in the [rubrics document](../03_assessment/rubrics_multimodal.md).

---

## Notes for Adaptation

- Suitable for both formative and summative assessments.
- Adaptable to different learner levels and contexts.
- Consider adjusting the rubric descriptors based on the specific task or learning context.
