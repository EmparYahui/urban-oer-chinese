# Design to Assessment Mapping — OER System Alignment

## 🧠 Purpose

This document ensures full alignment between instructional design and evaluation criteria.

## 🔗 Mapping logic

### Urban input
→ Assessed in terms of relevance and contextual awareness

### Mediation process
→ Assessed in terms of linguistic transformation quality

### Multimodal production
→ Assessed in terms of coherence and integration of modes

### AR contextualisation
→ Assessed in terms of spatial relevance and interpretive depth

### OER potential
→ Assessed in terms of reuse, clarity, and adaptability

- Mediation quality
- Multimodal coherence
- Pedagogical clarity
- Reusability (OER criteria)
