# Assessment — OER and Multimodal Language Production

This folder contains the assessment framework used in the Urban Linguistic Landscape OER module for beginner Chinese (A1–A2).

The evaluation system is designed to support both language learning and Open Educational Resource (OER) quality assessment.

---

## Purpose

To evaluate learner-generated artefacts in terms of:
- linguistic and intercultural mediation
- multimodal production quality
- linguistic competence development
- OER potential (reusability and adaptability)

---

## Assessment principles

Assessment is:
- formative and process-oriented
- aligned with task-based learning principles
- focused on mediation and meaning-making
- designed to support open educational practices

---

## Rubrics included

- Linguistic and intercultural mediation rubric
- Multimodal production rubric
- Linguistic competence criteria
- OER quality and reusability rubric

---

## Note

This framework operationalises "OER quality" as a pedagogical construct, linking language learning outcomes with open education principles (reuse, adaptation, and redistribution).
