## 🔁 Core teaching logic

Every lesson follows 4 steps:

1. **Context** → where learning happens  
2. **Task** → what learners do  
3. **Mediation** → how meaning is processed  
4. **Output** → what learners produce  

---

## 🧭 Step-by-step design process

### 1. Choose a context
Select one urban situation:

- street signs  
- shops  
- transport  
- public spaces  
- cultural locations  

---

### 2. Define one task
Choose ONE action:

- observe language in context  
- interpret meaning  
- translate or explain  
- create a response  

---

### 3. Add mediation
Decide how learners transform meaning:

- from L1 → L2  
- from image → text  
- from sign → explanation  
- from experience → interpretation  

---

### 4. Define output
Select one final product:

- short text (2–5 sentences)  
- bilingual caption  
- multimodal poster  
- annotated image  
- AR-linked note (optional)  

---

## 🧪 Example complete lesson

### Context
A Chinese restaurant sign in the city.

---

### Task
Learners observe and interpret the sign.

---

### Mediation
- Identify key words  
- Translate meaning into L1  
- Explain cultural or linguistic elements  

---

### Output
- 3 sentences in Chinese OR  
- bilingual caption explaining the sign  

---

## 🔧 Adaptation rules

### To simplify (A1 level)
- reduce vocabulary demand  
- allow L1 support  
- focus on recognition and basic meaning  

### To extend (A2 level)
- add explanation tasks  
- require full sentences in Chinese  
- include cultural interpretation
