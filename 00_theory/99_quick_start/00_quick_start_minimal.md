# 🚀 Quick Start (1-minute version)

## 1. Go outside
Find any Chinese text in the city (signs, shops, transport).

## 2. Take a photo
Capture one example of real urban language.

## 3. Understand it
Ask:
- What does it mean?
- What words do I recognize?

## 4. Create
Write:
- 1–2 sentences in Chinese OR
- a bilingual caption (Chinese + your language)

## 5. Share
Your work becomes an open educational resource.

---

## 🔁 Flow

Observe → Understand → Produce → Share

---

## ⚠️ No theory needed
Start immediately. Learn through action.
