# Multimodal Urban Trail

## Overview

This artefact was developed during the Jane’s Walk activities and documents elements of the urban linguistic landscape through geolocated multimodal entries.

Students combined photographs, location-based information, Chinese-language annotations, and cultural observations to create a publicly shareable learning resource.

## Learning Objectives

- Observe and interpret linguistic landscape elements.
- Develop mediation skills through contextual explanation.
- Create multimodal educational content.
- Connect language learning with authentic urban environments.
