# Multimodal Rubrics — Evaluation of Learner Artefacts

## 🧠 Overview

This document defines rubric-based evaluation for multimodal student outputs.

---

## 📊 Evaluation dimensions

### 1. Linguistic clarity
Accuracy and communicative effectiveness.

### 2. Mediation quality
Degree of meaning transformation across contexts or languages.

### 3. Visual integration
Coherence between text and visual elements.

### 4. Semiotic density
Effectiveness of combined meaning-making modes.

### 5. OER potential
Reusability and adaptability of the artefact.

---

## 🧠 System role

Provides consistent evaluation criteria for multimodal outputs across tasks.

---

## 📦 Output

- Standardised multimodal rubrics
- Evaluation consistency across artefacts
