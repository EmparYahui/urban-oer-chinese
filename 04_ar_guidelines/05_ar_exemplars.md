# 🧪 AR Task Exemplars

## 🧠 Overview

This document provides examples of AR-integrated learning tasks.

---

## 🧩 Example 1 — Urban sign mediation

### Task
Learners identify urban signs and reinterpret them in Chinese.

### AR function
- QR code triggers contextual information
- learners add translations and interpretations

---

## 🧩 Example 2 — AR city narrative

### Task
Learners construct a spatial narrative of the city.

### AR function
- geolocated artefacts
- multimodal annotations (text + audio + image)

---

## 🧠 Pedagogical focus

All tasks emphasise:

- mediation
- contextual meaning
- multimodal production
- spatial learning
