# 🧰 AR Technology Stack

## 🧠 Overview

This document outlines possible technological infrastructures for AR implementation.

The system is **device-agnostic and pedagogy-first**.

---

## 🧩 Core technologies

### Mobile AR platforms
- ARKit (iOS)
- ARCore (Android)

### Development tools
- Unity
- WebAR frameworks

### Lightweight alternatives
- QR codes
- geolocation maps
- web-based overlays

---

## 🧠 Hardware requirements

- smartphones (minimum requirement)
- tablets (optional)
- AR glasses (optional advanced layer)

---

## ⚠️ Principle

Low-tech implementations are fully valid.
